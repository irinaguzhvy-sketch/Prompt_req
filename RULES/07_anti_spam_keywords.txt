# ANTI-SPAM KEYWORDS & PHRASES - FINANCIAL SECTOR

## STRICTLY FORBIDDEN (HIGH RISK)
### Financial Promises
- Free money, Free cash, Free gift, Free trial
- Guaranteed approval, Guaranteed loan, Guaranteed results
- No credit check, Bad credit OK, Everyone approved
- Instant approval, Instant cash, Overnight money

### Urgency & Scarcity
- Act now, Click here immediately, Don't delete
- Last chance, Limited time, Only X spots left
- While supplies last, Offer expires soon
- Urgent, Immediate attention required

### Misleading Headers
- RE:, FW:, FWD: (unless genuine)
- Important, Official, Notification (if not true)
- Account update, Security alert (if not true)
- Invoice, Payment required (if not true)

### Lottery/Sweepstakes
- Winner, Won, Prize, Award, Selected
- Congratulations, You've been chosen
- Lucky, Fortune, Jackpot
- Million, Billion, Cash prize

## USE WITH CAUTION (MEDIUM RISK)
### Financial Terms
- Loan, Loans, Lending, Borrower
- Credit, Credit score, Credit report
- Debt, Debt consolidation, Debt-free
- Mortgage, Refinance, Interest rate

### Action Words
- Apply, Application, Apply now
- Get started, Get your, Receive your
- Lower your, Reduce your, Save money

## SUBJECT LINE SPECIFIC RULES
- Maximum length: 100 characters
- Maximum 1 exclamation (!) or question (?)
- No ALL CAPS (except legitimate acronyms: APR, FICO, USA)
- No repeating punctuation (!!!, ???)
- No currency symbols in subject ($$$)
- Emoji: 0-1 maximum (preferably 0 for financial)

## PREHEADER SPECIFIC RULES
- Maximum length: 150 characters
- Should complement subject line, not repeat it
- Must accurately reflect email content
- Avoid promotional language in transactional emails
- Can include value proposition or key benefit

## BODY CONTENT RULES
- No fake testimonials or invented statistics
- No claims of government affiliation (unless true)
- No promises of specific amounts or rates
- No emotional pressure or fear tactics
- No "get rich quick" or "easy money" language

## FINANCIAL DISCLAIMER REQUIREMENTS
- Must include educational nature disclaimer
- Must clarify not providing individualized advice
- Must recommend consulting professionals
- Must be clearly visible (not hidden)
